Our Works

Just a few of the places we had a part in designing.

Client: Work: House on the Hills

Terrance wanted a lovely lanai area to his living room in Hawaii Kai—an inviting extension of his home where indoor comfort meets island living. Our design focused on creating a relaxed yet refined outdoor space that seamlessly connects with the interior, using natural textures, durable coastal materials, and comfortable seating for gathering or quiet evenings. The result is a breezy lanai retreat that captures the spirit of Hawaii Kai living while enhancing the warmth and functionality of Terrances home.

Client: Work: Mikaie Vase

Mikai Vase is a shop in SALT Kakaʻako that we helped transform with a distinctive rustic-modern aesthetic. From the open storefront with large doors that welcome in natural light to the thoughtfully designed interiors, every element was curated to feel warm, organic, and contemporary. Even the bathroom carries the concept through with rich wooden textures and natural tones, creating a cohesive space that feels both modern and grounded—perfectly complementing the creative energy of Kakaʻako.

Client: Work: Florance Garage

Florence wanted a very modern feel for his garage that would complement the lush, forest-like setting of his open entrance in Kailua. Our design blended clean, contemporary lines with natural elements to create a space that feels both functional and connected to its surroundings. The result is a sleek yet grounded garage area that balances modern design with the calm, green atmosphere of Kailua living.
